How to Detect a Scam

  1. They contact you - No government agency will contact you first to tell you that you qualify to receive grants. Grant opportunities are announced through government agency websites, TV news or friends for grant seekers to find them, not the other way around.
  2. These types of information are available and free to access on government funding agency websites here.
  3. They pretend to be from the government - You may receive calls from people indicating that they are calling from a government agency. Remember, government does NOT call you to give you grants unless you apply for it here
